Live More Sustainably

Small changes, big impact. Discover daily tips to help the planet.

search

Switch to LEDs

LED bulbs use up to 80% less energy than traditional incandescents.

Ditch Plastic Bottles

Carry a reusable bottle to reduce single-use plastic waste.

Shop Local

Support local farmers and reduce the carbon footprint of your food.

Start a Compost Bin

Turn your food scraps into nutrient-rich soil for your garden.

Bike More, Drive Less

Reduce emissions and get great exercise by cycling for short trips.

Unplug Electronics

Save energy by unplugging devices that are not in use.

Plant a Garden

Grow your own food to reduce food miles and packaging.

Adjust Your Thermostat

Lowering your heat by a few degrees can make a big difference.

Recycle Properly

Learn your local recycling rules to avoid contamination.
